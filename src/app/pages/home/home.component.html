Weather App

This is my first complete sample work! This app's main purpose is to display my form capabilities: field validation & error handling.

NOTE: The street input is the only field that affects the resulting forecast. If it contains a season, the forecast will reflect that season. Otherwise, the forecast will reflect a random season. For a full breakdown, select the info button that appears in the upper-left corner of this app.

TLDR: Forecasts are randomized since this app serves to highlight my form abilities.
